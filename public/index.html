Camping Adventures


Camping is an amazing hobby that I have had the pleasure of doing for many years.
This page is dedicated to some of the amazing places I have traveled to over the years.

Benifits of Camping


◊ Getting to see the world ◊

Growing up my parents wanted us to have adventures and for them the best way was to take us out and see the world.
Thanks to that I had the chance to see some of most beautiful parks in the counrty. These were trips that to
this day I remember and hope to one day revisit when I get a chance.

◊ Getting to unplug ◊

In this day and age we have become extreamly connected. While that is amazing I know for me it can get to be to much.
One of my greatest joys is getting out to an amazing camp site and getting to put my phone in ariplane mode.
Granted I still keep it with me for photos or emergencies its still nice to get away from the world for a bit.

◊ Creates memories ◊

Even the little trips an hour down the road can create some of the best memories. One my most recent trips I went to
Ocala National Forest with a friend of mine and I will get to enjoy the memeories of us kayaking down the river
there for years to come. Not all camping trips have to be huge endevors.

My Favorite Camping Locations


Joshua Tree National Park

Joshua Tree was one of the first national parks I ever went to and it was to place that I learned to rock climb.
For the several years I lived on the west coast this beautiful park became a highlight of my summers.
Even after moving to the east coast I have still made the trip to visit.



Ocala National Forest

Ocala National Forest has been a favorite spot of mine since moving to florida. There is a river that runs through
the area just a short walk from most of the camp sites that is a blast plus Juniper Springs is one of the best
places to go and cool off during those long Florida summer days.



Sequoia National Forest

Sequoia National Forest should be on everyones to do list if you are making a list of parks to visit. I had the
best time hiking around and just exploring all this park has to offer. While walking down the trails in the forest
you truley get to experiance the peace and beauty that can be found outdoors.
